ABOUT SPARKMAN

Over 15 years ago, Doug Sparkman’s Magic “hobby” became so successful that he left his former management life behind and became a Full-Time Magician. He learned to entertain kids and families as he built his corporate entertainment business.

As of January 2014 he has performed over 2700 shows for kids and families and thousands more in the corporate world. He is one of the few magicians in the world that can entertain kids and adults alike.

Sparkman’s notable clients include, Ford Motor Company, First Tennessee Bank, Curb Records, Vail Resorts, The PGA Tour, FedEx, and many more.

Sparkman has performed from Memphis to Tokyo, Japan and many places in between.
